
I. Installation

1. Run MCRInstaller.exe. Must be a 64-bit computer.

2. the program itself is src\runGUI.exe or runGUI.m

II. what is saves


when it saves the columns are ordered like this:

1. MajorAxisLength - length of the long side (pixels)
2. MinorAxisLength - width of the short side (pixels)
3. Area - pixels
4. perimeter - perimeter
5. orientation
